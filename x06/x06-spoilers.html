Intersteller

2014

Starring

Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, Jessica Chastain

Quote

Cooper - Mankind was born on Earth. It was never meant to die here.

Summary

In the far future our planet Earth suffers from natural calamities and disasters. Now there is only one way to protect the existence of mankind which is Interstellar travel. A team of astronauts are sent to explore and find a new habitable planet. The story revolves around the team of astronauts, particularly Cooper and his duaghter Murphy. The father daughter relationship they share has a higher meaning which ultimately saves the human kind. How Love can break the barriers of time and space and connect two beings, is the moral of the movie.
